I'm Chris, and I'm thrilled to join the Terraform Enterprise team! I thought
it might be nice to share a some random things about the weirdo behind the new avatar in Slack!

- **Previously, I spent a formative part of my career at [Red Hat][1]**, where I worked on [ManageIQ][2], the
  open source cloud management platform behind [Red Hat CloudForms][3].
  The majority of my time there was working on issues related to Ruby and Rails
  specifically; ManageIQ makes heavy use of Rails internals (Some 'fun' examples:
  [1][4], [2][7], [3][8] ), and keeping it on the bleeding edge of Rails and Ruby
  development was a top priority. I was also the technical lead on ManageIQ's
  [GraphQL][5] API.
- **Before I was in software, I was an orchestral clarinetist**. I have lots of [really terrifyingly embarrassing pictures of myself holding a clarinet][12], too.
